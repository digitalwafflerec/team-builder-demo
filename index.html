Methodology. Salary benchmarks are drawn from the live Digital Waffle UK Salary Portal dataset: 298 roles benchmarked across 8 UK regions and 32 cities, built from Digital Waffle placement data calibrated against ONS earnings data and live advertised salaries, refreshed monthly. City figures apply a live local market adjustment to the regional benchmark; Remote (UK) applies the median within-role remote premium. Hiring demand reflects live vacancy counts. True cost adds employer National Insurance at 15% above the £5,000 secondary threshold and the 3% minimum employer pension contribution on qualifying earnings (£6,240 to £50,270). It excludes the Employment Allowance, bonuses, equity, kit and recruitment costs. Figures are gross annual salaries for permanent UK roles. Nothing you enter here is stored or sent.
